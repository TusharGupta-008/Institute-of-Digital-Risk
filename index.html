About IDR

Bridging Theory & Execution

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to managing digital, cyber, and technology risk roles.

We solve the talent gap by integrating academic insight with real-world active industry projects. IDR ensures practitioners are not just academically prepared—they are deployment-ready for tomorrow's AI landscapes.
