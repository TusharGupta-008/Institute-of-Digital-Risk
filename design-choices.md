# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) Design Notes

### Symbolism
The isometric cube logo symbolises structure, solid foundations, and multidimensional resilience. The three visible faces represent the intersecting disciplines IDR covers: academic insight, industry practice, and technological innovation. The clear geometric shape implies a systematic, robust approach to managing digital and AI risks.

### Colour Palette
The primary colour palette consists of a high-contrast triad designed to evoke authority and modern technical focus:
- **Off-White (#F3F4F6) & Deep Black (#111827):** These neutral, high-contrast tones provide a clean, academic, and institutional gravitas. 
- **Vibrant Orange (#FF5A00):** This accent colour injects a sense of vigilance, energy, and urgency, strongly aligning with the theme of digital risk and cyber resilience.

### Typography
"Inter" was chosen as the primary typeface for both the brand and the web presence due to its exceptional legibility at small sizes and strict geometric proportions. In the logo, tight letter-spacing combined with heavy font weights ("Digital Risk") anchors the brand, conveying strength and stability, while the lighter "Institute of" maintains a clean, minimalist hierarchy.
